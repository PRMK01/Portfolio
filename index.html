about

I am a self-taught programmer and UX/UI designer based in Warsaw. In my projects, I focus on creating responsive websites designed in a user-oriented approach. So far I have had the most opportunities to work with the frontend technologies - mostly JavaScript and React. But in the nearest future, I’m planning on extending my practical knowledge of the backend too. Being a full-stack developer is what I’m aiming for.

Other than strictly programming, I also like taking care of the graphic design on the website, so I have experience with programs like GIMP and Inkscape.

I enjoy working in a team environment, and I get along well with people.Logical thinking and the ability to solve complex problems are my fortes.
